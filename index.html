What do you miss the most about food from earth?

Astronaut Chris Hadfield in a conversation with Adam Savage and Jamie Hyneman from “Mythbusters“ has says:

“The food up here is good. But you miss the smell of food coocking because we just reheat everything in packages. And then you miss food with texture, a lot of our food is like baby food, so you miss something that's really crunchy!”
